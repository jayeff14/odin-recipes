Chicken Tenders

Description

An attempt to replicate the delicious Chicken Tenders from Elephant Bar. This homemade version of chicken tenders will have you remember the childhood memories of E-Bar.

This dish will have you frying small pieces of chicken like Abed in Community in no time and can set your chicken tender cravings for the week.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of Boneless, Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 4 Eggs Beaten
  • 3 Cups Panko
  • Canola Oil
  • Chicken Rub
    • 1 T Sugar
    • 1 T Salt
    • 1 T Pepper
    • 1 1/2 t Garlic Powder
    • 1 t Paprika
    • 1 t Dry Mustard Powder

Steps

  1. Toss Cut Chicken and Chicken Rub Togethers
  2. Put Flour, Egg and Panko in 3 Separte Bowls
  3. Coat Chicken in each bowl and refigerate
  4. Fry in medium-high heat until golden brown(note, internal chicken temperature should be 165 F)
  5. Transfer to strain to let chicken drain and cool off
  6. Enjoy!

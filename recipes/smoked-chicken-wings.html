Smoked Chicken Wings

Description

An easy smoked chicken wing recipe that will satisfy the family when you feel like having some bbq but not sitting around a smoker for 12 hours. This chicken wing recipe was inspired by a trip to Montgomery,AL where I tried some of the best smoked wings I've ever had. This inspiration comes from Moe's BBQ and hope that this recipe does it justice.

Ingredients

  • Party Chicken Wings
  • Salt
  • Favorite BBQ Rub (I prefer Meat Church's Holy Gospel for this recipe)
  • Melted Butter
  • Optional: Alabama White Sauce

Steps

  1. Dry Brine the Chicken Wings with Salt for 6+ hours in the refrigerator
  2. Start up or set smoker to 275 F
  3. At around 250 F, take out chicken wings from the fridge and apply generously your BBQ Rub
  4. Place on Smoker for 1 hour, flipping chicken wings after 30 minutes
  5. After 1 Hour, raise smoker temp to 500 F (or as close as possilbe), and flip wings about over to original side for 10-15 minutes
  6. Flip wings over, one last time for another 10-15 minutes or until chicken skin is crispy
  7. Take wings off the smoker and Enjoy!

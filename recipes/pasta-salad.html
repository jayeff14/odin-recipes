Pasta Salad

Description

A quick, easy and delicious Pasta salad to go with any protein that can also fill your carb and veggie fix.

Ingredients

  • Tri Colored Rotini Pasta
  • Italian Dressing (preferebly Olive Garden)
  • Vegetables
    • Carrots
    • Broccoli
    • Bell Peppers
    • Red Onion
    • Tomatoes
    • Sliced Olives
    • Etc.
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Water in Large Pot
  2. Slice Veggies into bite size pieces. Around the size of the pasta makes the veggies a little easier to pick up with a fork while you eat.
  3. Once water is boiling, add pasta and thicker veggies. Normally Carrots take the longest.
  4. As the pasta continues to cook, add Veggies in to continue cooking. Normally broccoli comes in next and bell peppers last, since they take the shortest amount of time too cook
  5. Drain pasta and veggies
  6. Allow pasta and veggies to cool down to room temperature and add remaining raw veggies (olives, tomatoes, etc)
  7. Add a generous amount of salad dressing and let it marinate in the pasta mix overnight or a few hours
  8. After marinated, add more dressing due to pasta soaking in orignal dressing and top with Parmesean
  9. Enjoy!
